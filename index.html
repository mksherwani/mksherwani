About Me

Welcome to my website! My name is Moiz, and I'm a Data / ML Scientist and computational biologist. With a Ph.D. focused on deep learning applications in healthcare imaging, my work bridges advanced computer vision techniques and biological data science. My work focuses on leveraging deep learning, graph neural networks, and computational biology to develop scalable solutions for precision healthcare and clinical decision support.

My expertise lies in medical image synthesis, graph neural networks for multi-omics integration, and real-world AI systems for diagnostics. I am passionate about building intelligent models that push the boundaries of healthcare innovation.
